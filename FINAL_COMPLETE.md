## 📊 Benefits of This Approach

### For Students:
- See all feedback without knowing specific peer identities
- Focus on content rather than who said what
- Less potential for bias or defensiveness
- Professional presentation

### For Peers:
- Freedom to give honest feedback
- No fear of social repercussions
- Encourages constructive criticism

### For You:
- Maintain full records (original CSV has names)
- Professional documentation
- Easy to regenerate if needed
- Consistent numbering (Peer 1 = same person in chart and reviews)

---

## 🔄 Consistency

**Important:** The peer numbering is consistent across each form:
- **Peer 1** in the chart = **Peer 1** in detailed reviews
- **Peer 2** in the chart = **Peer 2** in detailed reviews
- etc.

This means students can cross-reference between the visual data and written feedback while maintaining anonymity.
